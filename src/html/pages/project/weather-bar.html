Weather Bar

An Open Source Project

Weather Bar is a Weather Application that lives in your Menu Bar giving you beautiful access to real-time weather conditions and a generous 15-day forecast.

We honestly just made this app because we wanted it and could not find anything similar available online. This project gave us an excellent excuse to make something pretty and functional we could use every day.
